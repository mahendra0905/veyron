Meet the VEYRON Team: Your Personal AI Crew

VEYRON's real power comes from its team of expert AI agents. Each one has a special job, and they work together to get things done perfectly.

The Router (Master Agent)

The Router is the team leader. It figures out what you want and gives the job to the right agent.

The Empath (Emotional Agent)

The Empath understands your feelings. It helps VEYRON chat with you in a friendly and supportive way.

The Oracle (Web Search Agent)

The Oracle is your research expert. It finds info online, answers questions, and can summarize anything for you.

The Automator (Automation Agent)

The Automator handles your boring tasks. It can schedule jobs and build workflows to save you time.

The Watcher (Notification Agent)

The Watcher puts all your phone and PC notifications in one place so you never miss anything important.

The Mentor (Personalization Agent)

The Mentor learns how you work and teaches the other agents, making VEYRON truly your own personal AI.

System Requirements.

To ensure a smooth and responsive experience, please make sure your system meets the following minimum requirements.

Operating System

Windows 10 / 11

VEYRON is optimized for modern Windows features to provide the best performance and integration.

Processor

Intel i3 / AMD Ryzen 3+

A capable processor is needed for real-time language processing and multitasking without slowdowns.

RAM

8 GB Minimum

Sufficient memory allows VEYRON to run smoothly in the background alongside your other applications.

Storage Space

500 MB Free Space

This is needed for the application and to store your personalized settings locally on your device.

Microphone

Required for Voice

A clear microphone ensures VEYRON understands your voice commands accurately and quickly.

Internet Connection

Stable Connection

Needed for the AI to access the Gemini API and perform real-time voice-to-text conversion.
